1. Purpose This policy outlines the guidelines for using shared workspace areas to ensure a productive, respectful, and safe environment for all users. 2. Scope This policy applies to all individuals who use any shared workspace within [Company Name], including open seating areas, hot desks, collaborative zones, meeting rooms, and break areas. 3. General Guidelines Respect and Courtesy: Treat others with professionalism and respect. Keep noise levels appropriate and avoid disruptive behavior. Cleanliness: Users are responsible for tidying their area after use. Wipe down surfaces and dispose of trash in designated bins. Personal Items: Do not leave personal belongings unattended. Items left overnight may be removed. Food and Drinks: Allowed only in designated areas. All spills must be cleaned immediately. Confidentiality: Avoid discussing sensitive or confidential information in open areas.

4. Desk Usage Hot Desks: First-come, first-served basis. Clear all items at the end of each use. Assigned Workstations: If applicable, keep the area organized and free of clutter. Shared Equipment: Treat all devices and furniture with care. Report any damage or malfunction to IT or Facilities. 5. Meeting Rooms Must be booked in advance via the reservation system. Cancel reservations if no longer needed. Leave the room clean and reset to its default layout after use. 6. Technology and Power Use power outlets responsibly. Do not overload sockets. Do not unplug communal equipment or network cables without permission.

7. Safety and Security Keep pathways and exits clear at all times. Report any suspicious activity or safety concerns immediately. Follow all fire and emergency procedures posted onsite. 8. Violations Failure to follow this policy may result in: Verbal or written warnings. Temporary or permanent loss of access to shared workspaces. Disciplinary action as per HR policy. 9. Policy Review This policy will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary by the Facilities and HR departments. Approved by: [Manager/Director Name] Date: [Insert Date]
